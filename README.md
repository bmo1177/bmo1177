## 🧠 About Me

I am a Master's student at **Université Ibn Khaldoun, Tiaret**, specializing in Software Engineering. Following a two-year foundation in medical school, I transitioned into computer science to directly build the technological solutions shaping the future of healthcare. 

My primary research and development focus lies at the intersection of **MLOps, Edge AI, and Biomedical Computing**—specifically optimizing neural architectures for early neurodegenerative disease detection.

*   🔬 **Current Focus**: Lightweight Deep Learning (CNN-ViT hybrid architectures) & AI model optimization (ONNX Runtime) for medical imaging.
*   🌱 **Learning**: Deepening my skills in MLOps, Cloud Engineering, React Native, and Web3/Blockchain ecosystems.
*   📚 **Algorithms**: Actively sharpening my problem-solving skills via LeetCode & competitive programming (Python/JS).
*   💬 **Ask me about**: Java/Laravel architectures, medical AI applications, model optimization, or setting up automated local development workflows.
